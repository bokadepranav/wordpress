Garlic Ring Fryum

Description

The Garlic Ring Fryums that we present is processed with the help of automatic Italian plant under hygienic condition in 2D type. Having high crunchiness after frying and bitter taste, these have become preferred choices for a large number of children and adults. Widely used in homes, and restaurants, these have semi soft & hard texture and free from adulteration. Easy to fry and simple to digest, the Garlic Ring Fryums are available with us in a variety of packaging options and sizes from 30 to 50kg.
